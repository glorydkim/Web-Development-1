A Sleeping Totoro

Ingredients:
  • Rice
  • Black Sesame Seeds
  • Apples
  • Dried Sea Weed

Directions:

  • Grind the black sesame seeds into a course powder, then mix well with white rice.

  • Fill bento box with rice mix in Totoro shape.

  • Fill in Totoro's belly with white rice.

  • Trim the sea weed to make Totoro's stripes, whiskers, nose, and eye. Then place pieces onto Totoro as shown.

  • Slice apples and place around Totoro to fill empty space ^.^
