Rice Ninja

Ingredients:
  • Rice
  • Baby Carrots
  • Baby Zuchini
  • Avocado
  • Quail Eggs
  • Pea pods
  • Red Plumb
  • All Natural Food Coloring
  • Rice Seasoning
  • Ham
  • Dried Sea Weed
  • Green Leaf Lettuce
  • Black Food Marker

Directions:
  • Mix 1/2 cup of white rice and mix with blue food coloring. Fill bottom of bento box with blue rice, then sprinkle with rice seasoning.

  • Place green leaf lettuce in the bento box as shown.

  • Form egg shape with 1/4 cup of rice and wrap with seaweed. Use scissors to trim the seaweed to make the ninja's eyes. Then place the ninja in the bento box as shown.

  • Make 3 hard boiled quail eggs, steam 3 baby carrots and the pea pods, set aside the peas and carrots.

  • Peal the quail eggs and cut small parts of the egg white off to form the masks. Use the black food marker to make eyes, then place the eggs in the bento box.

  • Cut a slice of ham in the pagota shape as seen then place in bento box.

  • Cut a star in the skin of the red plumb and place in bento box.

  • Cut the carrot into the shape of a sword and place in bento box with the ninja.

  • Cut the baby zuchini in the shape of a star, then place peas, and carrots around the quail eggs and other space that needs to be filled ^.^
